OUR MISSION

Building a Healthier Nation Through Strength

Our mission is to contribute to the creation of a healthy nation by fostering citizens with balanced mental and physical well-being. We are dedicated to establishing branch associations at District and Provincial levels, appointing qualified instructors and judges, and conducting professional training workshops to popularize the sport. Furthermore, we aim to expand cultural sports and physique modeling arts while building strong relationships with international bodies to provide our members with scientific technical knowledge and opportunities for global competition.
